## Mobile Readability Guidelines

### Line Break Strategy
```jsx
// Add breathing room for mobile
<p className="text-lg leading-relaxed mb-6">
  The part-time CFO model has transformed UK finance leadership.
</p>

<p className="text-lg leading-relaxed mb-6">
  Companies now access senior finance expertise without full-time commitment.
</p>

// NOT this (wall of text):
<p className="text-lg">
  The part-time CFO model has transformed UK finance leadership. Companies now access senior finance expertise without full-time commitment. This creates opportunities for...
</p>
```

### Spacing Classes
- Paragraphs: `mb-6` (24px)
- Sections: `py-16 md:py-20` (64px/80px)
- Between H2 and content: `mb-8` (32px)
- List items: `space-y-3` (12px)

### Mobile-First Typography
```jsx
<h2 className="text-2xl md:text-3xl lg:text-4xl font-black mb-6">
  Part-Time CFO Day Rates UK 2026
</h2>
```
