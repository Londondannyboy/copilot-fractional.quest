Every `full_description` MUST include these 8 headers with the primary keyword in EACH:

```markdown

[2-3 paragraphs introducing the role and opportunity]

## What Does a {Engagement} {Role} Do?

[Core responsibilities and scope]

## {Engagement} {Role} Day Rates in the UK

[Rate information with context]

## Who Should Apply for This {Engagement} {Role} Role?

[Ideal candidate profile]

## {Engagement} {Role} Working Arrangements: Remote & Hybrid

[Flexibility and location details]

## UK Sectors Seeking {Engagement} {Role}s

[Industry breakdown]

## The UK {Engagement} {Role} Market

[Market trends and outlook]

## How to Apply for {Engagement} {Role} Roles in the UK

[Application process and CTA]
```

### Keyword Density Requirements

- Primary keyword (e.g., "Interim CFO") in ALL 8 headers
- Mentioned 8+ times in body text (aim for 15+)
- **Bold once** in body text (mid-paragraph, NOT near header)

## Example: Interim CFO Description

```markdown
## About This Interim CFO Opportunity in the UK

We're connecting experienced finance leaders with organisations seeking **Interim CFO** support across the United Kingdom. Whether you're stepping in during a transition, leading a turnaround, or providing cover for parental leave, these Interim CFO roles offer the chance to make immediate impact.

The demand for Interim CFOs continues to grow as UK businesses navigate economic uncertainty and seek experienced financial leadership without long-term commitment.

## What Does an Interim CFO Do?

An Interim CFO provides senior financial leadership on a temporary basis, typically 3-12 months. Key responsibilities include:

- Financial strategy and planning during transitions
- Cash flow management and working capital optimisation
- Stakeholder reporting (board, investors, lenders)
- Team leadership and mentoring
- M&A support, fundraising, or exit preparation
- Systems and process improvement

According to [ICAEW](https://www.icaew.com/), the role requires both technical accounting expertise and strategic business acumen.

## Interim CFO Day Rates in the UK

Current market rates for Interim CFO roles in the UK:

| Experience Level | Day Rate Range |
|-----------------|----------------|
| SME/Growth Stage | £800 - £1,100 |
| Mid-Market | £1,000 - £1,300 |
| Enterprise/PE-Backed | £1,200 - £1,500+ |

Rates vary by sector, complexity, and location. London-based roles typically command a 10-20% premium. Data from [Glassdoor UK](https://www.glassdoor.co.uk/) and our platform confirms these ranges.

## Who Should Apply for This Interim CFO Role?

Ideal candidates typically have:

- Qualified accountant status (ACA, ACCA, CIMA)
- 10+ years post-qualification experience
- Previous FD/CFO experience
- Sector expertise (tech, PE, manufacturing, etc.)
- Strong stakeholder management skills
- Proven track record in similar interim engagements

## Interim CFO Working Arrangements: Remote & Hybrid

Most Interim CFO roles offer flexibility:

- **Hybrid** (2-3 days on-site) - Most common for hands-on turnaround work
- **Remote-first** - Growing for tech companies and PE portfolio oversight
- **Full-time on-site** - Typical for intensive short-term engagements

The [CIPD](https://www.cipd.org/) reports that flexible working is now expected at senior levels.

## UK Sectors Seeking Interim CFOs

High demand sectors include:

- **Private Equity portfolio companies** - Investment period CFO cover
- **Tech & SaaS** - Scaling finance functions
- **Manufacturing** - Turnaround and restructuring
- **Healthcare & Life Sciences** - Regulatory compliance
- **Professional Services** - Partnership transitions
- **Retail & Consumer** - Cash flow management

## The UK Interim CFO Market

The market for Interim CFOs remains robust. According to [British Business Bank](https://www.british-business-bank.co.uk/) research, SMEs increasingly turn to interim executives during growth phases.

Key trends:
- Growing acceptance of interim as a career path
- Increased demand post-pandemic for financial resilience
- PE firms driving demand for portfolio company CFOs
- Technology enabling remote finance leadership
```
